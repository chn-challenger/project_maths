$\begin{align*}7\left(8x+22\right)+61&=607\end{align*}$$\\$

solution-text$\\$
$\begin{align*}7\left(8x+22\right)+61&=607\\[2pt]
7\left(8x+22\right)&=607-61\\[2pt]
7\left(8x+22\right)&=546\\[2pt]
8x+22&=\frac{546}{7}\\[2pt]
8x+22&=78\\[2pt]
8x&=78-22\\[2pt]
8x&=56\\[2pt]
x&=\frac{56}{8}\\[2pt]
x&=7\end{align*}$$\\$

10\left(x-2\right)&=40\\[2pt]
x-2&=\frac{40}{10}\\[2pt]
x-2&=4\\[2pt]
x&=4+2\\[2pt]
